This tool was made by Jamie Starke, a PhD Student in the CHISEL group at the University of Victoria.

The reason that I started this tool was based on my own experience. There were a few occasions where I needed to be able to look back at particular users Tweets, say, 6 months back. Unfortunately, after spending a few hours looking, I found that the existing tools out there (like Twitter's Advanced Search) just didn't do the trick, and were limited to about the past 30 days. This tool on the other hand is not limited to the past 30 days, and instead, limited based on the amount of tweets by the user. We are able to give you access to approximately the last 3200 tweets by any user.

I run this tool on the Google App Engine. The main reason for this was to allow me to get the tool out there, without a large server hosting cost. Of course, as a student, I don't have a lot of surplus money to devote to projects such as this.
